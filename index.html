Our Story

Roasted Over Fire, Served with Pride

Nelson's Lechon Ulo was born from one man's obsession with the perfect roast — crispy skin that shatters at first bite, meat so tender it falls from the bone. We specialize in lechon ulo (whole roasted pig head) and a full lineup of lechon classics.

We use traditional wood-fire pits, house-mixed spices stuffed deep inside, and no shortcuts. Whether it's a fiesta, a family reunion, or just a craving you can't ignore — we've got you covered.
